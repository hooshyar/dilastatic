Batumi View

Batumi View is a luxury hotel management mobile app designed to enhance guest experiences at a 5-star hotel apartment in Batumi, Georgia. With features like booking, food ordering, laundry services, and real-time support, Batumi View offers the ultimate convenience for discerning travelers.

Date

JUL 2023

Services

Mobile app development, resort management solution, Stripe payment integration

Scroll To Explore

The Challenge

The client required a comprehensive mobile app that would cater to their guests’ every need in a streamlined and intuitive way. The challenge was to develop an app that could seamlessly integrate services like room booking, food and laundry ordering, and customer support, all while maintaining the upscale feel of a luxury hotel experience. Additionally, the app needed to offer an admin panel for easy management of hotel operations, from room availability to guest services.

The Solution

Batumi View was developed as a feature-rich, user-centric mobile app that offers the following:

• Room Booking: Guests can browse available rooms and book their stay directly through the app.

• Food & Laundry Orders: Guests can easily place orders for food and laundry services with just a few taps.

• Real-Time Support: Integrated chat and call features allow guests to instantly contact hotel staff for any needs.

• Admin Panel: A separate admin dashboard allows hotel staff to manage rooms, monitor guest requests, and handle operations efficiently.

This intuitive solution seamlessly integrates every aspect of the guest’s stay, from booking to checkout.
